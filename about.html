About Me

“Love yourself. Be clear on how you want to be treated. Know your worth. Always.”
- Maryam Hasnaa

My name is Criselda, or you can call me “Isel”. I was born and raised in Meycauayan City, and I am currently a 3rd Year IT student at Our Lady of Fatima University - Valenzuela Campus. Initially, thank you for allowing me to introduce myself. I use my time management skills to work on my hobbies and interests. Some of my hobbies include watching anime. When I am not involved in my academic responsibilities, I am usually reading manga or drawing in my sketchbook. What is especially interesting about these hobbies is that they help develop my soft skills, such as critical thinking and creativity. I am excited to be here with you and can’t wait for us to learn together. Please feel free to approach me with any questions you might have.

This is what I love to do

Play Games

I enjoy playing a variety of video games. Open world titles like Genshin Impact and Honkai Star Rail captivate me with their immersive, expansive worlds and intriguing storylines. On the other hand, I also find tycoon games engaging, as they challenge my strategic thinking and management skills.

Travel

I love traveling to faraway destinations to find solace, allowing my mind to quiet down and my thoughts to unravel. Places like serene forests and tranquil beaches are my go-to choices, as they have a soothing effect on me, helping me relax and clear my mind.

Draw

When I have some free time on my hands, I find myself drawn to the world of art. It's incredibly captivating and has the power to ignite my creative spark. I frequently grab my sketchbook and start doodling, as it allows me to delve deeper into the realm of artistic expression.

Build Things

I love to build things that challenge my imagination. For example, I thoroughly enjoy creating ships and houses using popsicle sticks. These projects push me to become more creative and allow me to explore my creative potential.
